Lucie Guest Biography

Lucie Guest is an American actor, director, and writer who was named one of the Top Ten Filmmakers to Watch in 2017 by Independent Magazine. She is known for her recurring role in part 3 of the hit series “The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina,” premiering January 24, 2020, on Netflix.

Lucie Guest Career

Lucie has been in numerous film and television projects, including The CW’s “Supernatural” and “iZombie,” NBC’s “Timeless,” ABC’s “Dead of Summer” and “Beyond,” and BBC America’s “Orphan Black.”  Lucie has directed and written films that have aired internationally and won a number of prizes, including Best of Fest at the Women in Comedy Boston Film Festival, Best International Film at the Malibu Film Festival, and being named one of Kevin Hart’s Top 20 films for JFLs 2017.  Her most recent credits include directing LOVE AT FIRST FLIGHT, a feature-length romance she co-wrote, and FOR YOU, WITH LOVE, a rom-com.

Past credits include writing and directing the short film NEVER BETTER – A CLOSURE COMEDY as well as an off-beat comedic short film entitled BE RIGHT BACK. Lucie also created and directed CHARLIE AND YONI #LIFE AFTER 30 which was optioned by CBC’s Comedy Coup.
